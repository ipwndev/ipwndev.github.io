About Matthew Detmers

Matthew Detmers is a Compliance Manager at Radian Generation, where he specializes in renewable energy asset management compliance. With over nine years of experience, he has held key roles at Power System Resources and Cooper Compliance.

A graduate of California State University, Sacramento in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Matthew is recognized for his leadership in NERC compliance and his technical insight into regulatory and operational challenges in the energy sector.
