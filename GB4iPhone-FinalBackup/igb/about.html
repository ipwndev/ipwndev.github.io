About iGB
  • About the Emulator

    FOR BEST RESULTS: use iOS 4.1 OR HIGHER on iphone 3G or ipod touch 3G also press the + button and click add to home screen for full screen and more speed, except in iOS 4.3.X, it will be faster in mobile safari.

    iGB Began as a project to bring a Game Boy Emulator to iOS for my freinds who had not jailbroken their iPods.

    Do not ask me to upload ROMS I will distribute the source on completion for your server.

    Speed Test:

    iPod touch 4G: 8-16 FPS, iOS 4.2.1 iPad 2:25-30 FPS iOS 4.3.1 iPhone 4: N/A Mac: 10.6.7 FPS:60 PC: Windows XP: 45FPS
