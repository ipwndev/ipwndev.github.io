Boomer Band

What we play

We are a five piece band playing your favorite classic rock and R&B hits, along with some surprise “b” sides and newer hits, Boomer will have you and your friends up and dancing in “record time” with their booty-shakin’ beats, groovin’ guitars, cool keyboards, drivin’ bass, and not one, not two, but three superior singers. Bringing a crisp, new sound to those favorite hits from the 60s through today, their five members are new and old friends who love playing and have fun doing it, creating contagious fun that makes for a guaranteed good time!

Where we Play

We play shows around the greater Sacramento area including, Folsom, Orangevale, Fair Oaks, Citrus Heights, El Dorado Hills, Granite Bay, Loomis, Auburn, Roseville, Rocklin, Cameron Park, Placerville, Davis, Lincoln, Amador County, El Dorado Countys. If you are unsure if we can play in your area just ask on the contact page!
