Classic Rock and R&B

 
 

Playing your favorite classic rock and R&B hits, along with some surprise “b” sides and newer hits, Boomer will have you and your friends up and dancing in “record time” with their booty-shakin’ beats, groovin’ guitars, cool keyboards,  drivin’ bass, and not one, not two, but three superior singers.   Bringing a crisp, new sound to those favorite hits from the 60s through today, their five members are new and old friends who love playing and have fun doing it, creating contagious fun that makes for a guaranteed good time!
